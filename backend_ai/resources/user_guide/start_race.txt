To start a race follow these steps:
- go to a park. to find a park it is possible to use the HOME navigation button in the bottom left corner of the screen.
  from the map choose a park and click on the navigator button to get directions to the park.
- once in the park, look at a vehicle which has the dock's led light green.
- tap on the ScanQR button next to the HOME.
- scan the QR code on the vehicle.
- in the landing page you can read the resume of the vehicle chosen.
- by tapping on the "Avvia Corsa" button, blue on the bottom center of the screen, the dock will unlock the vehicle and u can start go wherever you want.
- on the next screen you can see the resume of the ride till now.
