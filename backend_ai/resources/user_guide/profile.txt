Guide to see the personal information of a MobiShare user.
Go to the profile section of the app (button right-end in the navigation stripe at the bottom of the screen).
You will see your profile picture.
Inside this section, you will find a button "Informazioni Personali" which allows you to display your personal information.
To logout from your account, you can use the "Esci" button located at the bottom of the profile section.
